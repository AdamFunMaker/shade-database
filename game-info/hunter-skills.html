BASIC HUNTER SKILLS

As a main action, you can rejuvenate up to SOUL OR MIND + Proficiency Bonus

As a main action, you can expend Spirit to rapidly regenerate your HP. You may heal up to a maximum of half your characters maximum HP.

As a free action, you can immediately call upon your Shade, make a sacrifice, the sacrifice must be sufficient for the Shade. If so the Shade can do one of four things:
-Rejuvenate all your SPIRIT
-Rejuvenate all your HP
-Rejuvenate the use of your Shade's Signature
-Any action that the player has in mind can still be suggested, but up to DM discretion
The more you sacrifice, the more it may demand next time if your bond is weak. Sacrifices are permanent, and can be another number of things that hold value to a character, body parts, memories, prized valuables, powers, companions (in certain cases), their own life

You may channel SPIRIT into your non-magical melee or non-magical ranged attacks as a bonus action, imbueing the weapon with SPIRIT reducing damage. In addition to the regular physical damage, the attack also does a quarter of the damage, up to a maximum of 8 as SPIRIT damage.

For 1 minute, you may be able to see outlines of fully formed Shades or distinguish the aftermath of a Shade ability from a regular magical/physical ability. This ability is not foolproof however, as powerful Shades can still circumvent it.

For 1 minute, you may be able to see outlines of fully formed Shades or distinguish the aftermath of a Shade ability from a regular magical/physical ability. This ability is not foolproof however, as powerful Shades can still circumvent it.

As a free action, a player may convert their spell slots into SPIRIT. The conversion is Twice the Spell Slot Level + PB, and a hunter can convert up to TWO spell slots per round. The SPIRIT generated this way goes over SPIRIT cap, but the generated SPIRIT decays at the end of the round.

[MONKS] As a free action, a player may convert their Ki into SPIRIT, they may only convert up to TWO Ki per round. With each Ki regenerating a value equal to half of their SOUL OR MIND stat.

As a bonus action, a player may deploy a blank domain with a minimum range of 5ft around the user. The user can increase the range of the domain by 5ft up to a maximum of 20ft, each 5ft increase costs another 2SP. The domain will participate in domain clashes, but is unable to dispel other domains, instead on a won Domain Clash, creatures inside the blank domain will not be affected by domain affects. Blank Domain only needs 3/4 rolled value. At the start of every turn between the two domain users, another Domain Clash will occur.
