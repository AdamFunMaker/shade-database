Shades
Skills Instruments Shades

Basic Shade Hunter Skills

1. Rejuvenate Spirit
As a main action, you can rejuvenate up to SOUL OR MIND + Proficiency Bonus
2. Rejuvenate Body [1SP/1HP]
As a main action, you can expend Spirit to rapidly regenerate your HP. You may heal up to a maximum of half your characters maximum HP.
3. Offering
As a free action, you can immediately call upon your Shade, make a sacrifice, the sacrifice must be sufficient for the Shade. If so the Shade can do one of four things:
  • Rejuvenate all your SPIRIT
  • Rejuvenate all your HP
  • Rejuvenate the use of your Shade's Signature
  • Any action that the player has in mind can still be suggested, but up to DM discretion
The more you sacrifice, the more it may demand next time if your bond is weak. Sacrifices are permanent, and can be another number of things that hold value to a character, body parts, memories, prized valuables, powers, companions (in certain cases), their own life
4. Channel Spirit [2SP]
You may channel SPIRIT into your non-magical melee or non-magical ranged attacks as a bonus action, imbueing the weapon with SPIRIT reducing damage. In addition to the regular physical damage, the attack also does a quarter of the damage, up to a maximum of 8 as SPIRIT damage.
5. Other World's Sense [1SP]
For 1 minute, you may be able to see outlines of fully formed Shades or distinguish the aftermath of a Shade ability from a regular magical/physical ability. This ability is not foolproof however, as powerful Shades can still circumvent it.
6. Exorcist's Circle
You spend 1 minute making the Circle, player can draw an exorcist circle that has a 30ft radius. Shades within the Exorcist's Circle will have their abilities weakened. They will have a -1 to their attack rolls and targeted DCs .
7. Convert Spirit
[CASTERS & NON-CASTERS]
As a free action, a player may convert their spell slots into SPIRIT. The conversion is Twice the Spell Slot Level + PB, and a hunter can convert up to TWO spell slots per round. The SPIRIT generated this way goes over SPIRIT cap, but the generated SPIRIT decays at the end of the round.

[MONKS]
As a free action, a player may convert their Ki into SPIRIT, they may only convert up to TWO Ki per round. With each Ki regenerating a value equal to half of their SOUL OR MIND stat.
8. Blank Domain
As a bonus action, a player may deploy a blank domain with a minimum range of 5ft around the user. The user can increase the range of the domain by 5ft up to a maximum of 20ft, each 5ft increase costs another 2SP. The domain will participate in domain clashes, but is unable to dispel other domains, instead on a won Domain Clash, creatures inside the blank domain will not be affected by domain affects. Blank Domain only needs 3/4 rolled value. At the start of every turn between the two domain users, another Domain Clash will occur.

Hunter Arts

1. Tread Upon The Emotions of the Other Side
When using Other World's Sense as main action, your expertise in spirit senses allow you to effectively gauge the flowing spirit of characters around you. As a main action, make a DC16 perception/insight check against a creature within line of sight and 60ft, on a success, you learn the creatures current amount of Spirit and HP
2. Superior Rejuvenatation of Body
It is now a free action to use Rejuvenate Body, maximum half cap is now removed. As a main action, you can also expend SP to Rejuvenate Body on targets other than yourselves if you can touch them. They heal HP according to your HP-SP ratio (martial/non-martial).
3. Channeled Precision
When using Channel Spirit, you can expend an additional 8SP to give the attack roll advantage, on a successful attack, half the damage of the attack is incurred as SPIRIT reducing damage. Cap on SP reductions is removed.
4. Everflowing Spirit Stream
By focusing and tapping into your inner SPIRIT, you start to let it flow throughout your system freely and into your blows. Channel Spirit is now a continuous effect. Once using a bonus action, all consecutive attacks will consume 2SP each and have the Channel Spirit effect. After each Channel Spirit usage, the user gains +1 to attack rolls. This effect stacks up to a maximum equivalent to your PB. The user may cancel the effect at any time.
5. Veil from the Banished
As a reaction, you can spend 2SP to quickly veil yourself in a blank domain, expanding it to only cover the surface of your body. Any domain ability that should affect you is instead nullified one time. This feature can be used a number of times equal to half your PB rounded down.
6. Spiritually Enhanced Defense
You gain the ability to channel and manifest SPIRIT to shield yourself from physical attacks. As a reaction, you can reduce damage you receive from an attack by expending your SP, decreasing the damage by the amount of SP you expend up to half your maximum SP and increases the SPIRIT DEF by +1.
7. Exorcising Aura
As a main action, you can use your SPIRIT to ethereally manifest an exorcist circle expanding out from you. The exorcist circle cannot move, and the circle disappears when the Shade Hunter who casted is either SPIRIT drained or downed.
8. Compacted Domain Technique
When using a non-blank domain expansion, You can choose to create the domain at a much smaller space, condensing it but strengthening your authority over that space. The domain expansion will only be 10ft radius and will ignore the effects of the blank domains. The space where the compacted domain and another domain overlaps will become neutral, and neither domain expansions effects will register in that area. The domain can still be forcibly clashed with as a main action. You can convert an active domain into a compacted one and vice versa with a main action
9. Body Before the Soul
In dire moments of need, the sheer physicality of the hosts body is able to surpass what even the soul is capable of. When using Rejuvenate Body, it no longer consumes SP. Rather as a main action, you restore HP equal to your missing SP from your max SP. This effect is tripled for Martials.
10. Naturally Otherworldly
Other Worlds Sense no longer costs SP and is a permanent effect on the host, after gaining enough proficiency in this ability, they are able to incorporate this sense into themselves naturally.
11. Rapid Collapsing Domain
When using a domain expansion, you can choose to hastily expand it but in an unstable manner. The domain's effects will apply as if the targets have been in the domain for one turn. The domain will quickly collapse afterwards, however, by expanding domain so quickly, the host expands the domain and resolves the effects of the domain FASTER than a domain clash can occur, bypassing a domain clash and a blank domains effects.
12. Punishing Circle
You spend 1 minute making the Punishing Circle, player can draw an exorcist circle that has a 60ft radius. Shades within the circle will have their existence slowly punished and judged. They have a -1 to attack rolls and AC. Additionally, for every round the Shade stays in the circle, they gain a -1 to SPIRT DEF, up to a maximum of -3.
