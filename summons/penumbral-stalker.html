PENUMBRAL STALKER
HP: 5 x Proficiency Bonus
AC: 12
SPD: 35ft
Drinker of Shadows
All SPIRIT damage dealt by Penumbral Stalker is converted into SPIRIT for the Host.
Eclipsed Slash
Make a melee attack against a creature within 5ft range, attack roll +WIS + Host PB. On hit, the creature takes 1d8+WIS psychic damage.
Abyssal Plunge [8SP]
Choose a creature within its movement speed range, move towards the creature and make an attack roll. Rising up high, and plunging itself straight into the ground into the shadows, Penumbral Stalker rends away at the creature, dealing 2d8+WIS psychic damage.
Draconic Shadow
Creatures that take damage from Penumbral Stalker have a -1 to their AC until the end of their next turn.
Watcher of the People (5SP/Reaction): [GUARDIAN ONLY]
As a reaction, the Penumbral Stalker can move up to half of its movement speed to another creature. It can then increase the creatures AC by PB. for one attack.
Castigate the Unworthy: [PUNISHER ONLY]
The stacks of Draconic Shadow can stack up to a number of times equal to PB, and lasts one additional round.
Serenity in Dusk: Śāma vica śāntī (14SP):
As a main action, the Penumbral Stalker fuses with the host, forming a shadowy hand around its left, and makes a melee attack within 5ft. Regardless of the remaining SPIRIT, host will suffer the effects of SPIRIT drain until the end of their next turn. On hit, the melee attack deals PBd12+WIS psychic damage, and reduces the enemy creatures AC by 5 until the end of their next turn. Afterwards, Penumbral Stalker is unsummoned.

As a Penumbral Stalker AND host reaction when host takes damage, if Penumbral Stalker is within 5ft range of the host, it will take the damage for the host instead. Immediately after, the host and Penumbral Stalker will instantly initiate Serenity in Dusk as part of the same reaction. If the Penumbral Stalker was reduced to 0 hitpoints by the damage it took for the host, it will give this attack advantage. After which, the effects follows as normal and Penumbral Stalker is unsummoned.

Guardian: 1
Punisher: 0
