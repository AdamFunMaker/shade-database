TWIN DARK SERPENT
  1. HP: 8 x Proficiency Bonus
  2. AC: 15
  3. SPD: 40
  4. COST: 4SP/Turn

Dark Serpent makes two attacks, one attack for each of its heads. Attack roll+ PB +Host WIS. 5FT range, First attack does 1D8+PB piercing damage, and the second attack does 1D8+PB psychic damage.

Dark Serpent is capable of grappling creatures with each of its heads, and can replace any of its attack actions with a grapple. Additionally, it has advantage on the grappling check, and uses host's WIS mod instead of STR for the grappling check. Dark Serpent automatically pents against grappled creatures.

Dark Serpent can make a counterattack against an enemy when it is hit by a creature within 5ft of it, either head can be chosen for this counterattack.

Dark Serpent makes an attack against a target within 5ft of it, it makes a contested STR check against the creature, on a success, the target becomes restrained until the end of the round. Regardless of whether the attack lands or not, the Dark Serpent is unsummoned and is treated as if it's killed.

As a bonus action, in a 90ft radius around where the host is, your shadow expands, and three marks appear near the side of your palm, every 6 seconds, one of the marks disappears. While in this domain expansion, your summons benefit from Delayed Vanishing without your reaction, and no longer cost SP to upkeep.
