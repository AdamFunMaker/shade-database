AVES
HP: 3 x Proficiency Bonus
AC: 14
SPD: 40ft
Flight: Aves is able to fly and carry one other character with it.
Wing Blade: Attack roll+proficiency bonus+Host WIS. 5FT range, deals magical physical damage equal to 1d6+proficiency bonus.
Nocturnal Senses: This creature has excellent senses geared towards thriving in complete darkness. It has advantage on perception checks in the dark and ignores difficulties on perception checks using vision.
Unveil True Form (4SP): Aves unmasks itself and any creatures that draw direct LoS other than the host to Aves face must make a WIS save or take 2d6 psychic damage. After which, the Aves is unsummoned and is treated as if it's killed.
