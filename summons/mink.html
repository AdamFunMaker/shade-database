ABYSSAL MINK
  1. HP: 1 x Proficiency Bonus
  2. AC: 16
  3. SPD: 30
  4. COST: 2SP/Turn

Advantage on DEX saves, and does not take any damage when successfully saving.

Select a target within line of sight and 30ft, the next melee attack against the character from an allied that is also within Abyssal Mink's line of sight, the character gains advantage on that roll.

Attack roll+PB+Host WIS 5FT range, deals 1d6+PB magical physical damage. If the target has Stalk Prey, the attack ignores resistances.

Attack roll+PB+Host WIS 5FT range. If creature has stalk prey and this attack successfully hits. the next attack roll against this creature has a +1d6 bonus.

Mink enters a shadowy state and hides within a shadow nearby, and instantly pounces from the dark and strikes at a creature within 30ft of it. This action can be held in which case it will stay within its shadowy state until the held action is released and gain's advantage on the attack. Within its shadowy state, Mink is invulnerable to damage. On a successful hit, the Mink deals a 1d6+PB damage type chosen by it's host, and the target gains vulnerability to that damage type for the next instance of damage. Regardless of whether the attack lands or not, the Mink is unsummoned and is treated as if it's killed.
