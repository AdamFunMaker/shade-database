SHADE OF DEATH
  1. HP: 75% of host MAX HP [94]
  2. AC: 12
  3. SPD: 30
  4. COST: 18SP

When Shade of Death takes damage and is killed, death will use its reaction to come back to life and become immune to that damage type for the remainder of its summoning duration.

The amount of HP sacrificed to be used as SP will reduce the damage of the next instance of outside damage by the same value, this ability stacks

Shade of Death makes a melee attack+PB+WIS against a creature within 10ft of it. It takes half of PB rounded upd12+ host WIS necrotic damage. If it is below half of its hitpoint maximum, instead of taking damage, the creatures hitpoint maximum will be reduced by that much instead.

As an action, Shade of Death attempts to crush a creature within 5ft of it in its arms. Make an attack roll WITHOUT MODIFIERS against the creature. The creature takes Half of PB Rounded Up D6 + host WIS necrotic damage. If the creature is below half of its hitpoint maximum, Shade of Death crushes them and pins them to the ground, the creature falls prone and takes an additional 2d6 Necrotic damage

As an action, make an attack against a targets Spirit Def stat, on a successful attack, Shade of Death tries to ethereally extract the soul out of a creature. The creature takes Half of PB Rounded Up D8+ host WIS psychic damage. If the creature is below half of its hitpoint maximum, the SP decreased by this ability is converted to HP for its host.

Every damage type that procs Shade of Death's Unreachable Demise, the host will receive resistance of that specific damage type.

As a bonus action of Shade of Death, designate a target within 20ft, it can no longer restore hitpoints until the end of their next round. Additionally Shade of Death gains advantage on attack rolls against it for two rounds.

As a free action of Shade of Death, it will toll a bell. Pick a target that it can see within 60ft, that creature is pulled closer to its demise for 1 minute. If the creature were to be reduced to 0 hitpoints during the next minute, instead of going down, the creature is instantly killed. Additionally, the creature cannot reduce the damage it takes in any way, through resistances, evasion, successful saves or otherwise. Shade of Death can only use this ability once per day, and once the character with the tolled bell is defeated, Shade of Death will be unsummoned.
