SHADE OF HEALING
A floating, angel with its body parts floating separately from one another. Above its head is a singular, gray halo, and its wings are segmented into multiple "feathers". The angel exudes a warm aura, presenting as a welcoming and kind spirit.

Blue and orange fire starts to wreath around the host as their eyes starts to glow distinctly blue and red. As a free action, you can enter The Hands That Burn Will Heal state, while in this state, half of all fire damage dealt by the host is converted to Fiery Life points. Additionally, when casting spells that restore hitpoints, the next instance of fire damage dealt by the host gains an additional value equal to half the healing as fire damage, this effect does not stack over multiple healing spell casts, and will instead take the most recent healing spell cast.

Fiery Life points represented by Vida's scar on Nico's body

As a bonus action, you can choose to remove one death fail from any creature within 40ft of you on your turn, when doing so, you can also choose to expend Fiery Life points to restore their hitpoints by a number equal to the amount of Fiery Life points you spend. Finally as a passive ability, all creatures within 40ft of you can reroll their death saves once per combat.

Once per long rest, when you restore hitpoints to a creature, you can refresh their usage of their signature. This works with Therapeftikí Fotiá, The Healing Flame. Additionally, as a passive, increase your SP cap by +5.

As a main action, target a creature within 120ft, you chant and release a serpent-like arrow of flame at the target, which will then explode in an explosion resembling a flower. Make a spell attack against that character, and make a spell attack against every creature within 40ft of it. You deal 4d12 fire damage to every character within range, you can pick a number of creatures equal to your PB, instead of taking fire damage, they will recover hitpoints instead. The fire damage from this cannot generate Fiery Life. This cannot generate Fiery Life

As a bonus action, you yourself can become blessed by the visage of Vida for one round. Any creature that attacks you with a melee attack, you can add a PB to your AC. If the attack misses because of it, you recover hitpoints equal to 2d12+PB. This cannot generate Fiery Life

As a main action, you can teleport up to 40ft away, bringing creatures within 5ft of you with you, costing 5 FL each.

By touching a character, you may seal any minor wounds on their body, provided no body part is severed or if the wound is no larger than one foot in diameter. Additionally, you can also calm the minds of characters in emotional distress.

When inspecting a character within 5ft of you, you can instantly discern how the wound came to be, e.g: Be able to discern what kind of weapon was used, if wound was caused chemically, spiritually or physically.

Whenever you expend SP, you regain hitpoints equivalent to the SP spent.

Whenever doing fire damage on your turn, recovers HP equal to proficiency bonus.

SHADE SIGNATURE

As a reaction, if an allied character within 40ft of you is downed or killed, you can use your reaction to wreathe them in flames, this character can be yourself. The flames deals fire damage to all hostile characters within 40ft of the user equal to your proficiency bonus + WIS OR SOUL mod, and the downed character is instantly healed for the damage that downed the character, and the effects of going down are reverted (i.e Instant failed death save)

As a bonus action, your entire self becomes a living carciature of fire, your Spirit rapidly forms a infernal layer around you. You become an unending inferno, and onlookers can distinguish you aside from your vague humanoid shape. This form lasts for two rounds, and while in this form, you are unable to lose hitpoints. Each creature you touch during this time will take damage equal to PBD12+PB fire damage OR regain hitpoints equal to PBD12+PB. At the same time, while in this form, all fire damage you deal ignores resistances, and your abilities no longer cost Fiery Life.

Host: Nico
