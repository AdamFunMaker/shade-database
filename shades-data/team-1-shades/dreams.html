SKILLS
Dreamwalker [5SP]
You can hop into the dreams of a character you see dreaming as long as your character is sleeping and within 100ft of the target. You will then walk amongst their dream without them knowing, in it, you will be able to interact and speak with the dreamer, but have no control over the dream itself. The dream reality may not behave proper rules of reality, -objects may not be destroyed, sudden drops in floors to an unending abyss. You may exit the dream, and if the dreamer wakes up, you and the Shade of Dreams will be violently jettisoned from it. Additionally, some characters may be aware of a Shade's powers due to its emittance of SPIRIT, and may figure out that it is a Shade attack. You can drag nearby sleeping creatures into another dream as long as they are within 100ft of the host as well. You can drag a number of creatures equal to your SOUL or MIND stat
Dreams Evade Reality [4SP/Round]
As a reaction, when an allied character except yourself fails a save or ability check within 25ft, you may add your WIS mod to the save.
Dreams that Never End
Shade of Dreams can manifest different types of dreams to warp reality and impose illusions that can fool even reality onto the battlefield. Neru enters a dreamlike state and is able to manipulate that battlefield from where he stands. These include:
  • Day Dreaming [7SP]
    Manifesting your day dreams into illusory reality, Shade of Dreams can deter attacks from hostiles. As a bonus action, pick an allied character you can see within 25ft. Until the end of their next turn, when a creature targets them for an attack roll, Neru can roll a WIS Saving throw against the enemy's save DC. On a save, the creature is unable to target them.
  • A Sunset That Never Ends [13SP]
    As a main action, you start to inspire hope and serenity into the souls and hearts of your allies. Pick a number of characters equal to your proficiency bonus within 25ft will gain max spirit equal to your proficiency bonus + half your level rounded up, these creatures also add a bonus equal to their proficiency bonus to initiative rolls and perception rolls, even if they already have proficiency. The effects last for 1 minute, this ability cannot stack.
PASSIVE: Dreamwatcher
As a passive ability with no SP cost, your character can vaguely hear/see the daydreams and wandering thoughts of people around them, but it's fairly fuzzy and random, almost impossible to glean exact details from it.
