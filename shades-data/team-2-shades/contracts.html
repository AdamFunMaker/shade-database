SHADE OF CONTRACTS
A shrouded four-armed figure with an ominous, expressionless mask. In each of the hands is a gavel, a balancing scale, a watch and a blank contract.

As a bonus action, you can create a contract for a willing, allied character within 50ft of you. The contract will impose a limitation on them, but bestow them an advantage. Pick one of the following restrictive effects:

-Movement speed falls to 0, if the character is moved in any way, even if involuntary, the contract will be broken.
-HP will be unable to be restored for the duration of the contract, and character will lose 20% of their max HP per turn.
-Character is unable to make any attacks or hostile actions against any creatures/entities or otherwise
-Character becomes unable to use SPIRIT or Shade abilities, and loses 20% of their maximum SPIRIT per turn.

Next, the character will gain one of the following advantageous effects:
-Movement speed is doubled for the duration of the contract
-Character gains advantage on all attack rolls
-Character regains SPIRIT equal to 3xPB per turn.
-Any effect up to DMs discretion. If the contract is broken by the restrictive effects being disobeyed by the target creature, the creature will suffer damage equal to 20% of their Max HP and 20% of their Max Spirit. The contract can be dissolved early as a bonus action but only after at least one round has passed since the contract's making. The contract terms will be loudly announced to all parties, including nearby characters. To maintain the contract, every round, the host's SP will decrease by 2.

As a passive ability, you have advantage on Insight checks pertaining to lies and deception. You are able to claw through those who seek to deceive others for their own gain.

When using Leges Ut Omnes Obedi, The Laws That All Obey, you can now specifically target a set number of creatures, instead of all creatures within the domain itself.

While Leges Ut Omnes Obedi, The Laws That All Obey is active, you can instead replace either a fine or a dividend with a spell cast instead. It does not consume your action economy, but still consumes your spell slot. If the spell requires a prerequisite attack roll, you make the attack roll as if you are able to, regardless of range or restrictions.

You are able to create a time bound contract with that creature. As a bonus action, you apply a contract to a creature you can see within 30ft. They gain one of the following effects after Trigger occurs:

TRIGGER: 2 of the characters turns end after time of cast.
EFFECTS: PICK TWO EFFECTS
-Gain SP equal to 3xPB
-Gain HP equal to 3xPB
-Immediately as this happens, character takes their next turn right after the contract is procced. This doesn't respect the alternating turn order rule
-Spellcasting once per turn restriction is removed for 1 round
-Movement is not restricted, and gain +10ft movement speed

You are able to create a soul bound contract with that creature. As a bonus action, you apply a contract to a creature you can see within 30ft. They gain one of the following effects after Trigger occurs:

TRIGGER: Character SP reaches lower than PB.
EFFECTS: PICK TWO EFFECTS
-Gain SP equal to 3xPB
-Gain HP equal to 3xPB
-Immediately as this happens, character takes their next turn right after the contract is procced. This doesn't respect the alternating turn order rule
-Spellcasting once per turn restriction is removed for 1 round
-Movement is not restricted, and gain +10ft movement speed. As a reaction, you can move without proccing opportunity of attack.

You are able to create a body bound contract with that creature. As a bonus action, you apply a contract to a creature you can see within 30ft. They gain one of the following effects after Trigger occurs:

TRIGGER: Character HP reaches lower than 3xPB.
EFFECTS: PICK TWO EFFECTS
-Gain SP equal to 3xPB
-Gain HP equal to 3xPB
-Immediately as this happens, character takes their next turn right after the contract is procced. This doesn't respect the alternating turn order rule
-Spellcasting once per turn restriction is removed for 1 round
-Movement is not restricted, and gain +10ft movement speed. As a reaction, you can move without proccing opportunity of attack.

You can only have ONE of each type of contract active at a time. When casting a contract, it lasts for 1 minute, and requires verbal incantation.

SHADE SIGNATURE

As a free action, you will manifest an ethereal domain that spans 90ft from you in a circle. The domain will no longer move once deployed, and can only be deployed from yourself. Shade of Contracts, will detach from you, going high up in the air, and binds a number of creatures of its choice to a contract. This ability when used is counted as a Domain for the sake of Domain Clashes.

Binding:
A set of rules that every character chosen within domain itself must abide by, only one law can be effective at a time, but as a bonus action you may change it on your turn.
-Characters don't miss an attack
-Characters are unable to use Shade techniques or abilities that use SPIRIT
-Characters are unable to move
-Discretion

When the rules are broken Contracts will impose a fine on the offender, a single creature can be incurred a maximum of 3 Fines in a round.
Fines:
Drain 5SP when breaching contract
Take 2d8 Necrotic damage when breaching contract
Next roll is rolled with disadvantage
Creature's reaction is confiscated

When a round is over, any character that did not receive a fine is awarded a Dividend
Dividends:
Regain 7SP
Next damage roll is rolled with pent
The creature can take one additional reaction
The creature is given one additional bonus action

The effects Dividends, Fines and who chosen by the contracts, cannot be changed and must be designated when the domain is laid out. ONLY ONE SET OF RULES/DIVIDENDS/FINES CAN BE ACTIVE AT A TIME

To maintain the domain, every round, the host's SP will decrease by 6. Additionally, the host is unable to regain SP while the domain is deployed. The host can use their main action to dispel the domain early.
