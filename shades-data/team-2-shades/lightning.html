SHADE OF LIGHTNING
Shade of Death resembles an expressionless, dark-shrouded creature. Within its dark shroud, it can produce hands that when touched, will drain the target of its life force and spirit.

As a free action, for every 10ft movement you moved during the turn, you gain a +1 to attack rolls, this effect remains until the end of your turn.

You can make an attack roll that cannot suffer from disadvantage or be reduced in any way.

Become as fast as the thunder, as a free action, you can double your movement speed.

Expending SPIRIT, you may manipulate the electricity flows in appliances that you directly touch, you are still able to manipulate the power and electricity of those attached to the appliance, but at greater cost of SPIRIT. The SPIRIT cost to manipulate electricity is DMs discretion, but as a basic benchmark, 10 SPIRIT allows you to turn on and off a high powered flashlight just by touching it.

Shade of Lightning can travel through electrical currents and emerge from any entrance, no matter how small, even the crevices of a phone. However, due to Shade of Lightning's nature, this is hardly a way to stealthily infiltrate a location to find information. Shade of Lightning can go up to 60m away from its host, before zipping back out of fear.

SHADE SIGNATURE

If using Thunderspeed in the same turn, the SP cost of this ability will be halved, rounded down. As a main action, strike an enemy within melee range with Pochita being imbued and charged from within the sheathe of your blade. Once striking this enemy deal, lightning is charged inside the enemy. You may then use your free action to sheathe your sword, causing lightning to instantly rupture and spark out from within the enemy, dealing 12d6 lightning damage. This ability cannot crit, and it will be a main action to draw your sword once more. This ability targets SPIRIT DEF instead of AC

Host: Ricochet
