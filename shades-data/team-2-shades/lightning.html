SHADE OF LIGHTNING
Resembles a little angry puppy made entirely of lightning sparks. It's overly energetic and zealous attitude often lands it in trouble, but it is an otherwise obedient little puppy dog. The luminosity of the lightning determines its mood, with happiness being indicated with a great overflow of electricity - to the point that anyone it touches is given a painful but harmless shock - or sadness/exhaustion being indicated with a barely noticeable spark from its hairs.

As a free action, for every 10ft movement you moved during the turn, you gain a +1 to attack rolls, this effect remains until the end of your turn.

You can make an attack roll that cannot suffer from disadvantage or be reduced in any way.

Become as fast as the thunder, as a free action, you can double your movement speed.

Expending SPIRIT, you may manipulate the electricity flows in appliances that you directly touch, you are still able to manipulate the power and electricity of those attached to the appliance, but at greater cost of SPIRIT. The SPIRIT cost to manipulate electricity is DMs discretion, but as a basic benchmark, 10 SPIRIT allows you to turn on and off a high powered flashlight just by touching it.

Shade of Lightning can travel through electrical currents and emerge from any entrance, no matter how small, even the crevices of a phone. However, due to Shade of Lightning's nature, this is hardly a way to stealthily infiltrate a location to find information. Shade of Lightning can go up to 60m away from its host, before zipping back out of fear.

Whenever you pent, you regain SP equal to your PB

As a bonus action, all your attacks this turn gain +PB to their attack rolls if you move more than 30ft. If the attacks pent, they instead crit.

SHADE SIGNATURE

If using Thunderspeed in the same turn, the SP cost of this ability will be halved, rounded down. As a main action, strike an enemy within melee range with Pochita being imbued and charged from within the sheathe of your blade. Once striking this enemy deal, lightning is charged inside the enemy. You may then use your free action to sheathe your sword, causing lightning to instantly rupture and spark out from within the enemy, dealing 12d6 lightning damage. This ability cannot crit, and it will be a main action to draw your sword once more. This ability targets SPIRIT DEF instead of AC

The weapon the host wields becomes charged with spiritual energy, emanating a fiery, volcanic glow and billowing ash from its blade. From the ashes, volcanic lightning starts to spark and flow, causing searing wounds in the enemies it slashes. As a bonus action, you can charge your weapon with volcanic and lightning energy. Your eyes start to glow orange akin to magma and lightning.

-Your attacks deal fire, lightning and thunder damage, any of your choice
-When you pent a creature with an attack, an arc of lightning will shoot out and strike another creature, dealing the same damage as the previous hit.
-When you pent, you instead crit.
-As a main action, you can end the signature, plunging the sword into the ground and letting loose a flurry of thunder and volcanic lightning, before slashing one last time. Every creature in a 15ft line in front of you will take 6d8 lightning or fire damage on a failed DC10 + PB DEX save, and half as much damage on a save.

As a free action, you create a binding vow with your sword, in exchange for borrowed power now, you forsake your mortal limits. Your body starts to surge with lightning, and you are able to surpass mortal limits, going faster than the speed of sound. You enter a near divine lightning state for 6 seconds (1 turn).

For this turn:
-Your movement speed is Quadrupled
-Your attack rolls benefit from 2xPB
-Creatures can no longer take reactions against you, as you act faster than they can humanly react. (Only reactions, held actions still g)
-Only while in this form, you can use another signature, excluding 建御雷; Avatar of Thunder and Blades.
-Your body takes a massive toll and is sacrificed in lieu for your spirit, when you use SP, you can instead expend your HP instead.
-You have an extra two actions this turn

Once the turn is ended, you take Character level D12 true damage, and you are unable to move and attack next round as your character. Additionally, you gain three levels of exhaustion.

Host: Ricochet
