SHADE OF NATURE
The shade of nature resembles a small humanoid sprite made of flowers, vines and leaves. It can manipulate its size, appearing as a much larger humanoid, standing as tall as 7ft, or standing as small as a house cat. Shade of Nature has an echoing, monotone voice, but comes off as a warm, compassionate spirit.
Voice of Nature: Advantage on animal handling and nature checks. Additionally, only Shade of Nature (not the host), can communicate with plants and animals, thought communication is limited.
Green Thumb (3SP): Shade of Nature can make vegetation grow rapidly, imbueing wilting plants and creatures with life force. Aside from that, animals feel a calming presence when Shade of Nature shows itself. Shade of Nature can also sprout smaller plants and vegetation from its body, such as simple flowers or vines.
Bloom: As an action, the flowers atop Shade of Natures head will change and shift. blooming into different types of plants for different effects. Shade of Nature can only have one Bloom active at a time.
Voice of Nature: Advantage on animal handling and nature checks. Additionally, only Shade of Nature (not the host), can communicate with plants and animals, thought communication is limited. Additionally, Shade of Nature's host gains affinity with the bodies and vessels of animals. If in wild shape or polymorphed, the host can still use Shade abilities that use SP without Shapeshifted Penalty.
Bloom: Tulip (8SP): A symbol a good luck and prosperity, as a bonus action, a creature within 60ft of the host has tulips sprout out from under neath them, blessing them with good fortunes. The creatures next failed roll will automatically be rerolled, but the second roll must be used.
Kaṁīpaṇa māṁ saḍō: Decay Into Nothing, And be Born Back into the Cycle (5SP/Round): As a bonus action, in a 90ft radius around where the host is, all creatures within the range will start to decay. Each living creature within the range will lose max HP equal to 1d8 + half of their level per turn. Shade of Nature can designate creatures within line of sight to have immunity to the HP decay. If a creature moves more than 20ft while under the effects of decay, the decay reduction will double. This ability counts as a Domain for the sake of Domain Clashes
