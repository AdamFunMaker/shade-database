SHADE OF PURSUIT
Manifests as a reptilian eye that appears on the skin of the host. To speak, it can manifest a mouth on the skin of the host. It is needlessly harsh on its host, and expects the most perfect and immaculate of combat performance from them

As an action, you can inspect a physical mark left by a creature (blood, prints, etc). Shade or Pursuit will be able to discern the direction in which they travelled within a range of 100ft of the original physical mark.

When successfully landing an attack on an enemy, you can expose it to a subsequent attack. The next attack roll you make against that target will be made with advantage.

If Shade of the Hunt deems the fight to be going well, you will be granted buffs, if Shade of the Hunt deems the Hunt to be going poorly, it will punish the Host. When gaining victories, you can choose to reroll buffs of the same tier when achieving a victory instead of taking a buff from a new tier, it will then replace that current tiers buff.

Shade of Pursuit's senses are sharpened beyond a point unparalleled by any creature or mortal means. As an added effect, it heightens the senses of its owner as well. Host has an extra +1 to to perception checks against creatures or entities it's after. Under specific situations, Shade of Pursuit may increase this perception bonus of the owner towards certain targets.

SHADE SIGNATURE

As a free action you are able to designate a creature for hunting for three rounds. Against this creature, your attacks ignore resistances and always roll with a bonus 1D6 added to the attack OR damage roll. While signature is active, you can obtain double the number of buffs from Trial of the Hunt, Cull of the Weak, up to 6 in total of your choosing. When the target creature is more than 50ft away, host character gains +20ft movement speed and is immune to movement speed reductions i.e grapples, difficult terrain, spell effects.

When any ally of the host kills a creature marked by this signature, regain spirit and HP equivalent to the killing blow, and the signature is cooldown is reset. The signatures buffs will remain for the remainder of the combat, unless explicitly said so. Signature ends when host is downed or incapacitated.

Host: Caine
