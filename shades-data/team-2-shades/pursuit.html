SHADE OF PURSUIT
Manifests as a reptilian eye that appears on the skin of the host. To speak, it can manifest a mouth on the skin of the host. It is needlessly harsh on its host, and expects the most perfect and immaculate of combat performance from them
SKILLS
Superior Tracker [5SP]
As an action, you can inspect a physical mark left by a creature (blood, prints, etc). Shade or Pursuit will be able to discern the direction in which they travelled within a range of 100ft of the original physical mark.
Bleeding Weakness [3SP/ATK]
When successfully landing an attack on an enemy, you can expose it to a subsequent attack. The next attack roll you make against that target will be made with advantage.
PASSIVE: Trial of the Hunt, Cull of the Weak
When gaining victories, you can choose to reroll buffs of the same tier when achieving a victory instead of taking a buff from a new tier, it will then replace that current tiers buff.
