SHADE OF VENGEANCE
Shade of Vengeance manifests itself as a little white cat. Despite its appearances, it is indeed the incarnate of vengeance, bringer of doom to wrongdoers, punisher of misdeeds, equaliser of hatred and is most definitely a very cool shade, or so it says
Blood of the Guilty (5SP): On a hit, the bullet absorbs the targets blood. The user may now see the target's past misdeeds (harming another being) through their point of view. The user is mentally alerted if the target inflicts damage.
Vow of Vengeance (8SP/Reaction): As a reaction, when a hostile creature within 40ft of you makes an attack roll against an allied creature, you can mark that creature for vengeance. If your next attack successfully lands, it will be treated as if it was a pent. You will gain disadvantage on a subsequent attack.
Collect Thy Penance: When dealing damage against an enemy that has successfully attacked an ally within 60ft, reduce the creatures SP by half your PB rounded up and regain that same value of SP. This ability can only be used a number of times equal to half your PB rounded up.
Retribution (5SP/Reaction): When any ally within 60ft is targeted by an attack, make an attack against the enemy dealing the attack. This attack does not proc any features or spells other than shade abilities and cannot receive damage mod bonuses. Retribution increases number of uses for Collect Thy Penance by one.
Nauyin Zunubanku Ya Shafe ku: The Weight of Your Sins Shackle You (10SP): On a successful hit, 3 shackles emerge from the ground and anchor its target. On a crit, the number of shackles increases to 4. For each shackle, target loses 5ft movement speed. When the target is targeted by an attack or the target makes an attack roll, you can add or subtract a d6 to the roll, when doing so, a shackle is broken.
