SHADE OF VENGEANCE
Shade of Vengeance manifests itself as a little white cat. Despite its appearances, it is indeed the incarnate of vengeance, bringer of doom to wrongdoers, punisher of misdeeds, equaliser of hatred and is most definitely a very cool shade, or so it says
SKILLS
Blood of the Guilty [5SP]
On a hit, the bullet absorbs the targets blood. The user may now see the target's past misdeeds (harming another being) through their point of view. The user is mentally alerted if the target inflicts damage.
Vow of Vengeance [7SP/Reaction]
As a bonus action, designate a creature within 60ft, they will gain Vow of Vengeance. Any creature that rolls a successful attack roll against the creature will be counterattacked by the host provided they are within range and capable. This counterattack can be used twice a round, and cannot benefit from any modifiers or feats. When successfully counterattacking, gain SP equal to your PB.
SIGNATURE
Nauyin Zunubanku Ya Shafe ku: The Weight of Your Sins Shackle You [12SP]
On a successful hit, 3 shackles emerge from the ground and anchor its target. On a crit, the number of shackles increases to 4. For each shackle, target loses 5ft movement speed. When the target is targeted by an attack or the target makes an attack roll, you can add or subtract a d6 to the roll, when doing so, a shackle is broken. When the host's attack roll pents against the target, provided they do not expend a chain, a new chain appears and latches onto the target creature.
