SHADE OF VENGEANCE
Shade of Vengeance manifests itself as a little white cat. Despite its appearances, it is indeed the incarnate of vengeance, bringer of doom to wrongdoers, punisher of misdeeds, equaliser of hatred and is most definitely a very cool shade, or so it says

As a main action, the host throws a chain that emerges from the ground underneath the host's feet. The chain travels in a straight line of 30ft, make an attack roll against each creature in the line. On a successful hit, deal 1d10+PB bludgeoning damage and their next attack roll is subtracted by d8. On a crit, the target is grappled until the end of their next turn. If a creature is affected by The Weight of Your Sins Shackle You, when hit by this ability one shackle is added to them, and another Condemn the Sinners will occur, originating from the creature again.

As a main action, the host throws a binding of multiple chains at a 5ft square within 30ft range. From where the binding of chains land, they rapidly extend and bind hostile creatures within 30ft. The creatures must save on a DC8+PB+WIS STR saving throw or be pulled into the center of origin. Creatures who fail the save will take 1d6 damage per creature pulled to the center. If a creature is affected by The Weight of Your Sins Shackle You, is affected by this ability, they fail the STR saving throw immediately, and immediately one shackle is added to them and all adjacent hostile creatures.

As a bonus action, designate a target within 30ft that has incurred damage to your allies within the last turn. Pun dives into the ground and vanishes, a shining, fiery feline spirit appears above the target. For a number of times equal to PB, allies who land a successful attack roll against this target regain SP equal to PB. If the marked creature is affected by The Weight of Your Sins Shackle You, when any shackle on the target is used, host regains 2SP.

On a hit, the bullet absorbs the targets blood. The user may now see the target's past misdeeds (harming another being) through their point of view. The user is mentally alerted if the target inflicts damage.

When you overflow past SP cap, only half the excess SP decays instead of all of it.
Additionally, when you land crits, you regain SP equal to 2x your PB.

As a bonus action, designate a creature you can see within 60ft, within this round, they must either:

-Lose one break bar
-Be defeated

If predicted right, you regain the use of your signature, if wrong, that creature gains +1 times use of their signature.

This ability does not cost SP, but can only be used once per long rest.

SHADE SIGNATURE

On a successful hit, 3 shackles emerge from the ground and anchor its target. On a crit, the number of shackles increases to 4. When the target is targeted by an attack or the target makes an attack roll, you can add or subtract a d6 to the roll, when doing so, a shackle is broken.

As a bonus action, an ethereal revolver with a monochrome edge manifests in your hand, this revolver has six shots, and deals the same damage die as your regular revolver.

When firing this revolver, you character can flip a coin to turn the favour of lady luck to their side. Once making the attack roll, you can choose to expend 2SP to reroll the attack roll, you can do this an infinite amount of times.

For every reroll you do, your next attacks with this weapon deal an additional +1 to attack and damage rolls. When firing the last bullet in the chamber, this effect doubles.

Additionally, this weapon automatically crits on a roll with mods of 26 or higher and deals triple damage dice on a crit.

Host: Colt
