SHADE OF DEATH
Shade of Death resembles an expressionless, dark-shrouded creature. Within its dark shroud, it can produce hands that when touched, will drain the target of its life force and spirit.

When doing damage to creatures (spell saves/attacks) below half of their HP maximum, they take additional damage equal to your proficiency bonus. You regain HP equal to a quarter of the damage dealt. Additionally Shade of Death also benefits from this passive when he is summoned

Shade of Death is able to accurately discern the time of death of any deceased creature when touching any parts of its body. Additionally, Shade of Death can detect any creatures close to death i;e below half their maximum HP within 400ft.

When the host character is drained of SP, the host character HP is instead supplemented as the SP, and is converted to SP to prevent SP drain effects.

Death Vows proc Unstolen Demise. When below 25% maximum HP, Death Vow's no longer cost SP or HP. but will continue to proc

For every 25HP the host is missing, the Shade of Death will take an additional turn, taking turns up to a maximum of hosts PB . For every 25HP Shade of Death is missing, Grimm receives 1D12 HP per end of Shade of Death's turns.

As a bonus action, the next spell you cast that deals damage, you can change the spell's damage to slashing. Additionally, the cleave will spread and slash all around the target, target takes additional PBD6 slashing damage. Creatures with AC below 12 will take double the bleed damage. Each spell can only incur the effects once per creature.

As a bonus action, the next spell you cast that deals damage, you change the spell's damage to bludgeoning, the spell will ignore resistances. Additionally, they must make a STR save or fall prone instantly. Any structure, building or object will also take double damage from the spell. Each spell can only incur the effects once per creature. You can choose to expend more HP/SP to strengthen the Crush, for each extra stack, the enemy's save DC increases by 1 and they take an additional 1d12 bludgeoning damage. This stacks a maximum of up to your PB

As a bonus action, the next spell you cast that deals damage, you change the spell's damage to piercing, ignores resistance. The spell attack gains advantage when specifically targeting parts of a creature (limbs, eyes, body parts). On a successful pent, the spell instead crits. On a crit, this reduces the enemy AC by a value equal to your PB

You can only use this on spells that use attack rolls, it targets SPIRIT DEF instead of AC. As a bonus action, the next spell you cast that deals damage, you change the spell's damage to psychic. When successfully dealing damage with the spell, you regain HP equal to half the damage dealt.

SHADE SIGNATURE

As a bonus action, you physically manifest Shade of Death It follows the ASI of your base character and takes it turn right after the host characters turn. When Shade of Death is summoned, the host character can no longer regain SPIRIT, and when SPIRIT is drained, the host character can choose to consume HP instead, at a 1 for 1 HP to SP ratio.

This ability is usable by both Mahoraga or the host when below 50%HP when making a successful attack roll against the creature. The attack will deal necrotic damage equal to the missing amount of max HP AFTER HP/SP cost of the ability. This ability cannot decrease your HP past 1. After the signature is used, Unreachable Demise will be procced for the user and raga, gaining necrotic damage immunity and recovering HP to full. This ability can be used once per long rest
